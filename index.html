MYCARZ RENTALS - CAR RENTAL AGREEMENT

Owner: Joris Jordan Holdings (MYCARZ RENTALS)

Legal Jurisdiction: United States

1. IDENTIFICATION OF THE RENTAL VEHICLE

Owner hereby agrees to rent a passenger vehicle identified as follows: Make and model, Year, VIN, Color, and Plate Number (hereinafter referred to as "Rental Vehicle").

2. RENTAL TERM

The Owner hereby agrees to rent the Vehicle to the Renter for the duration specified in the application checkout details. The term of this agreement commences upon the pickup date and terminates upon return. Extension requests require a minimum of 2 days' notice prior to scheduled return; failure to notify may result in immediate request denial or additional administrative penalty fees.

3. MILEAGE

The Rental Vehicle has unlimited mileage per day within the agreed regional boundaries.

4. RENTAL FEES

Base fees are weekly. Weekly payments shall be due every Friday. The first payment is due once the rental agreement is signed by the client. Should the Renter fail to remit payment by 11:59 PM EST on the due date, a late fee of $50 shall be assessed. In the event payment is not received within 24 hours of the due date, the Owner reserves the right to disable and/or repossess the Rental Vehicle through the services of a security company.

5. RESPONSIBILITY FOR DAMAGE OR LOSS; REPORTING TO POLICE

You are responsible for damage to, or loss or theft of the Vehicle, including the cost of repair, or the actual cash retail value of the Vehicle if not repairable, plus loss of use and administrative expenses. You must report all accidents or incidents of theft/vandalism to us and the police immediately upon discovery.

6. INSURANCE COVERAGE & CLAIMS

Option 1: You agree to provide your own auto insurance covering yourself, us, and the vehicle. You must list the rental company as an interested party on the policy.

Option 2: Purchase Renters Collision Protection (RCP) from the rental company for $20/day. RCP pays collision damage to the rented vehicle up to $20,000 with a $250 deductible. RCP is non-refundable.

Option 3: You agree to allow the rental car company to create you a personalized insurance policy covering the vehicle.

Insurance Claims: In the event of vehicle damage, the renter is responsible for paying a $1,000 insurance deductible. If a claim is not filed, the renter agrees to pay the Owner directly for repairs.

7. OUT OF STATE DRIVING & SMOKING

Out of State Driving: Strictly prohibited. Driving outside the state terminates the agreement, and the renter assumes full responsibility for all associated towing fees, mechanical issues, or damages incurred outside state lines.

Smoking: Absolutely no smoking inside the vehicle. A smoking clean fee of $150 will be charged if any evidence of smoking is detected.

8. RETURNING VEHICLE

If the renter returns the vehicle mid-week (on or before Wednesday), the payment for the week will be prorated ( renter owes half of the weekly rate). If the renter returns the vehicle any day after Wednesday, they remain responsible for paying the full weekly rate.

9. BREACH OF CONTRACT

Includes failure to pay rental services, unauthorized use of vehicle, failure to return vehicle, negligence/misuse, allowing unauthorized drivers, and violation of traffic laws.
